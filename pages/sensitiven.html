Individual consulting procedure

Each being is different has different preferences and responds differently to each method. If you are not sure, what is right and the best for you, we'll find it out together.

possible procedure of a sensitive spiritual consulting session

  • We'll start with a short medical history and a short discussion, why you came to me. We will talk about your expectations and jointly define the goal of the session.
  • If you are not familiar with the divination and spiritual healing methods, I will give you a brief introduction to the different options.
  • Depending on your choice we will then continue with either a divination method or a guided meditation, a chakra analysis, a healing session, a (gemstone-) massage, akupressure or akupuncture, defining a Bachflower-mix, a food consultation or just talk.
  • At the end we will wrap up the session about the process, how you are and if your expectations and goals have been met.
  • Because this is an onsite consulting service and we define the process and goals together, there is no money back warrany.
  • On your request only I will provide you a written report with a possible complementary treatment suggestion.

Important

I do not promise to heal you, I am neither a medical doctor nor a promoted natural practitioner. If you have serious physical problems please visit a medical doctor.
