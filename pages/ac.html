process for a basic animal communication consultation

  • If you do not visit me in person, please pay in advance.
  • After your payment, please send me a picture of your animal by email (astrid@commuhnicat.com) or whatsup (+41 76 665 87 57). Additionally I will need the date of birth (if known) and sex of your animal.
  • Please formulate your questions as precise as possible (to avoid misunderstandings) or describe the problem (by email or whatsup)
  • After the completed communication with your animal I will contact you by email or whatsup to arrange a conversation with you.
  • The conversation with you will only take place after the communication with your animal so that I can get in contact with your animal as unbiased as possible.
  • Depending on your requested service I will create a written report and send it to you (including a possible complementary treatment suggestion).
